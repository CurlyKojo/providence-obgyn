At a glance

Pregnancy, trimester by trimester.

A quick visual of the visit cadence, tests, and conversations we'll walk through together. The full guide goes deeper — this is your map.

First trimester

Weeks 1–13 · steady beginnings

Visit cadence: every 4 weeks
  • Week 8 · First visitFull intake, early ultrasound, dating confirmation.
  • Week 10 · NIPT screeningCell-free DNA if elected — reviewed together.
  • Week 12 · Nuchal scanOptional, and we talk through the choice.
  • ThroughoutNausea, fatigue, early anxieties — welcome conversations.
Second trimester

Weeks 14–27 · the gentle middle

Visit cadence: every 4 weeks
  • Week 20 · Anatomy scanDetailed ultrasound · gender if you want to know.
  • Week 24 · Fetal movementKick-counting begins; we talk about how.
  • Week 26 · Glucose screenGestational diabetes testing.
  • ThroughoutNutrition, travel, sleep positions, birth plan drafting.
Third trimester

Weeks 28–40 · gathering close

Visit cadence: every 2 weeks, then weekly
  • Week 28 · Tdap & RhoGAMVaccinations and Rh-negative care.
  • Week 36 · GBS swabGroup B strep screening.
  • Weeks 36+ · Birth planFinal review · hospital tour · bag list.
  • Week 40 · We're thereDelivery at Summerlin Hospital with Dr. Symone.
